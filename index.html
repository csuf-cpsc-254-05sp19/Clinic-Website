About

I am a Computer Science student at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF) and my interests include software engineering, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. I currently work as a Supplemental Instruction Leader for the Computer Science Department at CSUF and I'm doing research under CSUF Associate Professor Dr. Gofman. Over the summer, I worked as a Software Engineer Intern at Rockwell Collins in Cedar Rapids, Iowa.

A few hobbies of mine include playing the guitar, working on personal projects, and learning new things. Whenever I seen an opportunity to learn, I make sure to take it. Every time I begin a new project, I make sure it incorporates something I haven't done before.

Restaurants & Bars

  • THE COVE RESTAURANT

    The resort’s all-day dining restaurant, The Cove, presents an eclectic selection of dishes inspired by international and Asian cuisines to be enjoyed indoors or in an outdoor setting. Signature dishes include our chefs’ creative take on steamed, barbecued and wok-fried Chinese and Thai favourites, Siamese egg noodles, and fresh seafood with tangy dips; while both a la carte dining and tempting themed buffets are available for breakfast and dinner. Vegetarian dining options are also available.

  • MARE ITALIAN RESTAURANT

    Dine on the authentic flavours of classical Italian cuisine in the evenings, savour uncomplicated Italian fare for lunch, and enjoy Mare’s award-winning wine list. Mare's Italian Chef, Chef Matteo Verini, offers to indulge you in exciting dishes that feature exquisite local produce and some of Italy’s finest imports either indoors or al fresco, with a view over the beach. Vegetarian and healthy options are also available. With its own wine cellar, high ceiling and décor dominated by warm colours, the private room at Mare is the ideal dining venue for groups of eight and for exclusive dinners.

  • BAREFOOT BAR

    The essence of ‘barefoot fun’, the Barefoot Bar, framed by the Andaman Sea and the resort’s Splash Pool, is perfect to linger over a glass or two. From late morning to starry night, you can sip from our selection of signature cocktails, including Mojitos prepared in many different ways, frozen Margaritas, the create-your-own Bloody Mary kit, and other concoctions that feature indigenous fruits and condiments. The expansive drink list also features a selection of wines and bubbly, as well as mocktails. You’ll also enjoy a light menu of salads, tapas, cold cuts, freshly landed seafood and grilled meats.

Projects

Formal Qualifications Testing

Created and implemented tests for the Rockwell Collins Tactical Targetting Network Technology (TTNT) Modem Hardware Abstraction Layer (MHAL). Used C++ and C# to write the tests.

Parking Predictor

Developed a system that can accurately predict parking availability at CSUF. Utilized Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn, and Keras to create and test various machine learning models.

Gif Bot

Developed the backend of a web application that will respond to queries in gifs. Utilized the Natural Launguage Tool Kit (NLTK) to obtain applical gifs through the Giphy API.

Photo Booth

Created a program that detects and applies various filters over users' faces. Utilized the Raspberry Pi camera module to take photos of the users and a Haar feature-based cascade classifier to detect the faces.

Conversation Bot

Programmed a chatbot that assists users in finding the best parking location at CSUF. Notifies user which location has the most amount of available parking spaces.
